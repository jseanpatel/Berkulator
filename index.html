What is all this?

Balancing your academic life with everything else college life has to throw at you can be a little difficult sometimes. As a Calhacks 6.0 team full of first semester students here at UC Berkeley, grasping an idea of how much classes can take up your time was and is still a challenge. Many students often times run into a problem of overloading themselves with academics and club activities which can lead to poor health management. By giving these students a quantification tool, Berkulator, they are able to see how difficult their semester will be given all the responses and thoughts people gave on various Berkeley databases.


How it Works:

1. Input your anticipated courseload and extra-curriculars.
2. Click "Generate" to recieve a Berkulator score.
3. Compare that score to suggested ranges to predict rigor.
